Time to start your summer reading.

How It Works

Read

A fiction book from the New York Times Best Sellers list is assigned on the 1st of the month. You will have the entire month to read at your own pace. Please borrow titles from the library or purchase on your own.

Discuss

Discussions are scheduled at month end and take place at the Bay Shore Neighborhood Library. Discussion questions are emailed the week prior. We encourage most of the book to be read in order to attend.

Repeat

Our summer program covers one pre-selected fiction book per month, from June to September 2022. In the fall, we will repeat a similar schedule with a new set of books. You may attend as many discussions as you wish.
